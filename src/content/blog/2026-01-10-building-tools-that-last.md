The best tools are quiet. They handle the boring parts and stay out of the way.

## What I optimize for

- Fewer moving pieces.
- Clear ownership of data.
- Easy migrations when things change.

## A simple checklist

- Does it still work without a build step?
- Can I explain it in one paragraph?
- Does it remove more pain than it adds?

The answer should usually be "yes" across the board.
